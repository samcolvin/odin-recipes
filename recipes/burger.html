Best Smash Burger

Making Smash Burgers can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:


Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Buns
  • Your favorite beef seasoning

Steps:

  1. Combine your beef and seasoning in a bowl
  2. Seperate into 1/4 pound patties
  3. Heat griddle to 450 degrees
  4. Use parchment paper to press the burgers on the grill flat
  5. Place burgers on a bun and serve with your favorite sauce
