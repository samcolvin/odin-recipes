Best Soup

Making soup can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:


Ingredients:

  • Chicken Broth
  • Your Favorite Noodles
  • Chicken

Steps:

  1. Cube and Boil Chicken
  2. Cook Noodes in Broth
  3. Combine Chicken and Noodles
  4. Add seasoning to taste
  5. Simmer 30 minutes
